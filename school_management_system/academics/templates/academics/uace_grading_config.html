Subsidiary Subject Grading

Subsidiary subjects (General Paper, Sub-Math, Sub-ICT) use Pass/Fail system

Pass: Numerical grades 1-6 (Distinction or Credit) → 1 point
Fail: Numerical grades 7-9 (Pass or Fail) → 0 points
Note: Subsidiary subjects do not receive A-E letter grades like principals. They use Pass/Fail only.
