Frequently asked

Questions head teachers ask us first.

Does SomaMange handle PLE, UCE and UACE grading correctly?
Yes. Every scale — Primary 1–7, UCE D1–F9, UACE A–F — is built in, including PLE aggregates, best-8 divisions and UACE principal + subsidiary points.
Can we import students from our existing Excel sheets?
Our onboarding team imports your students, classes, streams and opening fee balances for you. Most schools are running within a week.
What about internet in upcountry schools?
SomaMange is lightweight and works on modest 4G. Mark entry and attendance screens load in seconds on a basic laptop.
How secure is our data?
Student and financial data is encrypted in transit, access is role-based, and every login is logged. Only your authorised staff can see your school.
Who owns the data?
You do. You can export students, fees and marks as CSV or PDF whenever you like.
Can parents and students log in?
Yes. Parents see their own children's grades, fees and attendance. Students see their own timetable, report cards and e-learning.
